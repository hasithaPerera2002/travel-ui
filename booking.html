Located in Ella in the Badulla District region, with Ella Train Station and Ella Spice Garden Cooking Class nearby, Hillcrest Ella has accommodations with free Wifi and free private parking.

Some accommodations include a balcony and a satellite flat-screen TV, as well as a seating area.

The daily breakfast offers continental, American, or Asian options.

The bed and breakfast has a terrace.

If you would like to discover the area, hiking is possible nearby, and Hillcrest Ella can arrange a car rental service.

Demodara Nine Arch Bridge is 3.4 miles from the accommodation, while Hakgala Botanical Garden is 31 miles from the property. The nearest airport is Mattala Rajapaksa International, 53 miles from Hillcrest Ella, and the property offers a paid airport shuttle service.
